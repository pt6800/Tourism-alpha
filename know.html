About Our China Journey

Embark on an extraordinary 18-day journey through the wonders of China with our expert team at Wanderlust Adventures. This immersive experience takes you on a captivating exploration of China's rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and ancient traditions.

Our journey begins in the bustling metropolis of Beijing, where you'll delve into the heart of China's vibrant capital city, exploring iconic landmarks such as the Forbidden City, Tiananmen Square, and the Great Wall.

From Beijing, we venture into the tranquil beauty of rural China, where you'll witness the enchanting landscapes of Guilin and Yangshuo, with their iconic limestone karsts and winding rivers. Immerse yourself in the timeless traditions of rural Chinese life as you interact with local communities and experience authentic cultural activities.

The journey continues with a visit to Xi'an, home to the world-famous Terracotta Army and the ancient city walls. Here, you'll step back in time to explore China's rich history and marvel at the architectural wonders of the past.

As our adventure draws to a close, we conclude with a visit to the modern metropolis of Shanghai, where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with contemporary culture. Explore the vibrant neighborhoods, savor the delectable cuisine, and experience the dynamic energy of this cosmopolitan city.

Join us on this unforgettable journey to discover the beauty, diversity, and magic of China, and let the spirit of adventure guide you every step of the way.

Embark on an unforgettable journey along the majestic Great Wall of China, exploring its ancient watchtowers and breathtaking landscapes.

Experience the natural beauty of Guilin and Yangshuo with a scenic boat cruise along the tranquil waters of the Li River.
