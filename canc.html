TRAVEL MATE CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans may change, and you may need to cancel your booking. Please review our cancellation policy below:

1. Cancellation Period: Cancellations made more than 7 days before the scheduled departure date will receive a full refund.

2. Cancellation Fees: Cancellations made within 7 days of the scheduled departure date may be subject to a cancellation fee.

3. No-shows: If you do not show up for the scheduled tour without prior notice, no refund will be provided.

4. Refund Process: Refunds will be processed within 10 business days from the date of cancellation.

For any further inquiries or to initiate the cancellation process, please contact our customer service team at support@travelmate.com.
